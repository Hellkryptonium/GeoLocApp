All Features

GeoLocApp is designed to make your life easier, greener, and more connected. Explore all the powerful features that help you pin, share, and remember the places that matter most.

Pin Locations

Easily save your favorite nature spots, cafes, or secret hideaways with a single tap. Organize your pins with custom names and revisit them anytime.

Location Alarms

Set smart reminders for any location. Get notified when you’re near a pinned place—never forget to pick up groceries or visit a friend again!

Share & Collaborate

Share your favorite locations with friends and family. Plan group trips, coordinate meetups, and collaborate on adventures together.

Offline Access

Access your saved locations even when you’re off the grid. GeoLocApp works offline so you’re never lost, even in the wildest places.

Privacy First

Your data stays on your device. We never track your location or share your info—privacy and security are built in from the start.

Eco-Friendly Design

Inspired by nature, GeoLocApp uses a green, cozy interface and is optimized for low battery usage—so you can explore longer and greener.
